How to Forward Your Data

You can set up data-forwarding from your Octoblu connected devices by setting up device subscriptions or by sending messages to a forwarder device

1 Setting Up Device Subscriptions

You can use forwarder.octoblu.com to set up message subscriptions to your IoT connected things. The forwarder application will walk you through the process of
  1. selecting a datastore
  2. Setting the configuration options for connecting to your datastore
  3. Selecting one or more devices
  4. Selecting a Gateblu instance to run your forwarder

    We run the forwarder as a managed process inside Gateblu. Gateblu is Octoblu's Internet of Things gateway that allows you to connect your smart-devices, motors, sensors and more to Octoblu.

Once the wizard is complete a new forwarder device is created and is granted permissions to receive messages that are sent to or received by your selected devices.

It also notifies your Gateblu instance that it needs to run an instance of your forwarder device. Direct messages or broadcasts that are sent to/from your devices

Logging in to the forwarder app

1. Navigate to http://forwarder.octoblu.com in your web browser. You will see a login screen.

You will need your Octoblu UUID and Token as authentication credentials for the site. Every Octoblu account has a UUID and Token that can be used as a username/password to authenticate with Octoblu.

2. Go to https://app.octoblu.com/profile and copy the UUID displayed on the page

3. Generate Token on the Octoblu profile page
